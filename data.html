Data Downloads

Download the public CSV files used by this prototype. These files are research navigation data; official archival descriptions and images remain with MOFA and JACAR.

Core Tables

public_records.csv One row per public archival record. record_tags.csv Record-to-tag join table. public_tag_labels.csv Tag IDs and public display labels. public_search_index.csv Search index used by the website. review_flags.csv Open review flags for ambiguous historical entities. manifest.csv List of downloadable files, row counts, and sizes.

Controlled Vocabularies

controlled_vocabulary_regions.csv Regions and regional scope notes. controlled_vocabulary_entities.csv States, historical states, territories, and political entities. controlled_vocabulary_organizations.csv Organizations, institutions, and diplomatic missions. controlled_vocabulary_events.csv Conferences, sessions, announcements, and historical events. controlled_vocabulary_blocs.csv Political blocs and negotiating groups. controlled_vocabulary_keywords.csv Topical keywords and search terms. controlled_vocabulary_analytical_contexts.csv Interpretive research contexts.

How To Use

Use public_records.csv as the main table. Join it to record_tags.csv by record_id, then join tags to public_tag_labels.csv by tag_type and tag_id.

The controlled vocabularies explain preferred labels, scope notes, historical names, and review cautions. For official archival images and descriptions, follow the MOFA and JACAR links in the records.

Citation / Use

This dataset is a prototype research navigation dataset. Please cite the official archival institution for primary sources, and treat this site's CSV files as an interpretive finding aid.

Data source: Japanese Diplomatic Archives, Ministry of Foreign Affairs of Japan, and JACAR metadata where noted.
